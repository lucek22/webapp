# ProjectScarletSummerSprint

## Steps to set up camera

1) Place the ArUco Marker

    Lay the ArUco image flat on another level surface.

2) Stand on the Same Plane

    Position yourself so your feet are on the exact same surface as the marker.

    Your foot’s arch should be roughly parallel to the ground.

3) Stand Naturally and Evenly

    Distribute your weight through the middle of your foot.

    Avoid standing on your toes or heels, as this will distort measurements.

4) Hold Still and Capture the Image
