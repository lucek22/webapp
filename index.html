Reference Targets & Guides
Tibial Inclination (Shin Forward Tilt)
●
Optimal (≥ 38°): Full squat capacity; no upstream compensation expected.
●
Functional (30°–38°): Minor limitation; monitor under fatigue.
●
Restricted (< 30°): High compensation risk.
●
Asymmetry Risk: A difference of > 5° in tibial inclination between sides is a primary predictor of lower limb athletic injury.
